The plugin integrates your website with embed. embAd is an awesome free service that helps website owners to embed ads into their website.

== Description ==

Embed and control ads in your site using a powerful drag & drop toolbar. 

Don't know what is embAd? Check out our [video](http://youtu.be/BPb7m9cKk5U).

http://www.youtube.com/v/BPb7m9cKk5U?hd=1

== Installation ==

1. Register to [embAD service](http://www.embad.com/user/register). 
1. Upload the plugin to your blog, Activate it, then enter your PID and WSID keys you got when registered to embAD.
1. Log in to [embAD](http://www.embad.com/user) and click on "edit ads".
